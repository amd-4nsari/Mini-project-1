Courses Offered


Our college offers a diverse range of course selection categories, enabling students to choose programs that align with their interests, skills, and career goals.

BCA / BCA (Hons./ By Research)

BCA/ BCA (Hons./By Research) is an advanced academic program that focuses on providing students with a comprehensive understanding of computer applications and equips them with the necessary skills to conduct independent research in the field.

Master of Computer Applications

MCA focuses on the study of designing and developing real life IT Applications. This 4 semester program including cloud computing, Python, Internet of things, Android Applictions, JAVA, .NET, C# and many more

B.TECH
(COMPUTER SCIENCE & ENGINEERING)

B.Tech in CS & CSE at GLA is future focused program . This 8 semester program Including AI, Data Science, Cybersecurity, Cloud & DevOps and more

M. Tech (Computer Science & Engineering)

CSE is a rapidly evolving field covering topics like algorithms, programming languages, and software/hardware design. Rooted in mathematics, electrical engineering, and linguistics

Ph.D.
(Computer Applications & computer science Engineering)

This is 3 year course.The Ph.D. programme in Computer Science & Engineering is centred on inculcating the students with attitude, skills, and knowledge to conduct world-class computer science & engineering research.

PG DIPLOMA

There are many Diplomas i.e PG diploma in cyber Threade and security, diploma in Artificial Intelligence & Machine learning, diploma in Web and application development




Our Global campus


Spread across 110 acres of land, the university is home to over 20,000 students enrolled in a variety of professional courses. Well-designed and maintained buildings, contemporary laboratories, spacious residential complexes and recreational facilities make the campus one of the best in the region and gives its students an edge over their counterparts coming from other universities. The diverse student population makes it hard to describe the typical student and even harder to describe the quintessential GLA student experience. GLA is committed to making educational opportunities accessible to all by granting scholarships and financial aid to its students. Extracurricular, co-curricular and athletic opportunities, in addition to academics, GLA students are active around and beyond campus.
